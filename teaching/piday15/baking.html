Baking Competition

Have the best pie around? Have the most π pie? Submit yours to our pie baking competition, hosted by the MATRIX math club!

This is a friendly and casual event. Here are a few rules:

  1. Home-baked pies (or π themed foods) only
  2. Register at the following link before March 6: competition registration
  3. Bring your pie(s) to Altgeld Hall room 239 by noon March 14
  4. We cannot accept pies that require refrigeration or heating
  5. Submit your pie(s) in a disposable pie plate
  6. Submit a list of ingredients with your pie(s)
The actual judging process will depend on the number of submissions. Judging criteria will include appearance, taste, texture, and overall appeal. Separately we will judge creativity and use of the π theme.
